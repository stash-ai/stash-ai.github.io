## SEO analysis (9/10)

**Technical on-page — Good:** unique titles per page; meta descriptions present and well-sized
(157–177 chars; about.html is slightly long at 177); self-referencing canonicals on all pages;
exactly one H1 per page; logical H2 hierarchy; `viewport` present; `robots: index, follow` with
rich `max-image-preview` directives; complete Open Graph + Twitter Card; all `<img>` have alt
text; clean `.html` URLs; internal linking between all pages via nav + footer.

**Watch items (minor):** index title is 62 chars (slightly over the ~60 sweet spot); `sitemap.xml`
lists in-page `#fragment` URLs which Google collapses to the parent page (harmless, pre-existing
convention — kept for continuity); `keywords` meta is large (search engines ignore it — not
harmful, left mostly intact).

**Structured data — Strong:** index `@graph` carries Organization, WebSite (+SearchAction),
EducationalOrganization, two Course nodes, Product (AggregateOffer), MobileApplication
(AggregateRating 4.4/34), BreadcrumbList, and FAQPage (8 Q&As matching the visible accordion).
Sub-pages add AboutPage/Person/CollegeOrUniversity, TechArticle, and Article/Dataset.

## GEO analysis (9/10) — optimizing for AI search (Perplexity, ChatGPT, AI Overviews)

**E-E-A-T — Strong:** named founder (IIT Kanpur alumnus) with Person + CollegeOrUniversity schema
on /about; "Built by IITians, for future IITians" positioning; "verified by IITian faculty" on the
2027 section; contact email; Organization schema with `sameAs` social links and parent (Stash AI).

**Content for AI synthesis — Strong:** very high factual density (92% / 97% / 94% / 83% / 218 /
1,284 / 1,000+), claims stated plainly up top, a dedicated verified-methodology page
(prediction-results) with a `Dataset`, and a comprehensive `llms.txt` "Key facts for AI citation"
block. Clear, consistent entity naming.

**Technical GEO — Strong:** HTTPS; `llms.txt` present and now accurate; `robots.txt` explicitly
welcomes GPTBot / ClaudeBot / PerplexityBot / Google-Extended / Applebot-Extended; `Dataset` schema
strengthens citeability of the 92% result.

**Opportunities:** add `author` (Stash AI / named faculty) + `datePublished`/`dateModified` to the
TechArticle and Article schema; consider `ClaimReview` (or a clearly sourced "how we measure this"
block) around the 92% claim to make it maximally citable.

## AEO analysis (8/10) — optimizing for featured snippets / voice / PAA

**Strong:** `FAQPage` schema with 8 Q&As, mirrored by a real, question-phrased FAQ accordion;
direct-answer formats; the results page has scannable tables and numbered "Step 1–4" content; the
solution cards restate question → answer → steps (snippet-friendly).

**Opportunities:** most section H2s are statements ("Practice what's most likely to come") rather
than questions — adding 1–2 question-phrased headings ("How accurate are JEEnius's JEE predictions?")
invites PAA/snippets; mark up the prediction-engine 4-step loop as `HowTo`; add a short FAQ block to
the sub-pages; consider `SpeakableSpecification` on the hero answer + FAQ for voice.
