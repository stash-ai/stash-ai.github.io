About us

Building an AI tutor every JEE aspirant deserves

JEEnius AI exists to give every student — not just those who can afford elite coaching — a tutor that understands the JEE the way paper setters do.

Our mission

India's toughest entrance exam rewards depth, not memorisation. Yet most aspirants prepare with static question banks and one-size-fits-all coaching. JEEnius AI was built to change that: an AI tutor trained on 20+ years of JEE Main (2002–2025) and JEE Advanced (2007–2025) previous year questions — capable of explaining any problem step by step, predicting what's likely to be tested, and adapting to each student's weak chapters.

Who builds JEEnius AI

JEEnius AI is built by Stash AI, founded by an IIT Kanpur alumnus who lived the JEE grind first-hand. The team pairs that exam intuition with modern AI research — orchestrating leading reasoning models and fine-tuning them on authentic JEE papers and Kota-level notes rather than scraped summaries.

That combination powers our prediction engine, which was independently tested against all 1,284 questions of JEE Main 2026 and reached 92% topic coverage.
