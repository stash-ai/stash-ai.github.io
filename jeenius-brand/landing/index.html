A charged ring in a magnetic field that's switched on. Find the angular acceleration.

ANSWER
α = qB / 2m
1 Find the induced electric field
A changing B creates a circular induced field E along the ring, giving a tangential force on the charge.
2 Apply torque = Iα
The torque from that force equals the moment of inertia mR² times the angular acceleration α.
