Step 3 · Trap engine

Engineering the distractors

Real NEET questions are defined by their wrong options. Our template & distractor engine recreates the traps examiners love.

Misconception traps

Bait answers built from common conceptual slips.

Near-miss biology

Options from terminology mix-ups, classification slips or "halfway" reasoning.

Boundary bait

Edge-case answers focusing on limits, degeneracies or extreme values.

Partial-solution lures

Answers that match the step just before completion.

Step 4 · Prediction loop

From blueprint to validated paper

1

Blueprint generation: syllabus hierarchy + weightage forecasts decide the topic mix.

2

Drafting: GPT-5 and Gemini models craft the stem, solution and tone.

3

Scrubbing: o-series and Fathom AI audit units, diagrams, logic and difficulty.

4

Validation: similarity guards keep the set original, not recycled.
